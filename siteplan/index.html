🗺️ Lagos Heritage Hub - Website Plan

1. Site Name

Name: Lagos Heritage Hub

This name was chosen to represent a central platform dedicated to showcasing the cultural, historical, and modern heritage of Lagos, Nigeria.

Optional Domain: lagosheritagehub.org (availability not yet checked)

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a digital guide and archive for Lagos — featuring tourist attractions, local traditions, historical insights, event listings, and a membership form for cultural enthusiasts.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I visit to experience real Lagos culture?
  • How do I connect with local tour guides or join cultural events?

4. Color Scheme

  • Primary Color (#1E3A8A) - Used for headings and backgrounds.
  • Secondary Color (#FBBF24) - Used for accents and buttons.

5. Typography

Heading Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif

Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
