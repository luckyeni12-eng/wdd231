NP Membership — Benefits

NP Membership is free for qualifying non-profit organizations. Benefits include:

  • Networking & volunteer matching
  • Invitations to community events
  • Access to basic training workshops

Bronze Membership — Benefits

Bronze members receive:

  • Listing in our business directory
  • Access to member-only events
  • Discounted event tickets

Silver Membership — Benefits

Silver members receive Bronze benefits plus:

  • Training discounts and workshops
  • Featured directory spot
  • Opportunities to sponsor events

Gold Membership — Benefits

Gold members enjoy premium benefits:

  • Premium home-page spotlight & advertising
  • VIP access to events and private networking
  • Customized training & partnership opportunities
